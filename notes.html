Chapter 1: Generation of Computer

1️⃣ First Generation (1940–1956)

First generation computers used vacuum tubes. They were very large, consumed more power and generated heat. Example: ENIAC

2️⃣ Second Generation (1956–1963)

Transistors replaced vacuum tubes. Computers became smaller, faster and reliable.

3️⃣ Third Generation (1964–1971)

Integrated Circuits (ICs) were introduced. Operating systems were developed.
